Incident 1:
Location: Boiler Area
Gas: 84 ppm
Temperature: 61 C
Cause:
Gas leakage during Hot Work.
Result:
Explosion occurred.

Immediately stop Hot Work when gas exceeds 70 ppm.

Incident 3:

Worker entered Boiler Area without Hardhat.

Result:
Head Injury

Recommendation:
No worker should enter hazardous zones without PPE.

---------------------------------------------------

Factory Act Guideline

Hot Work shall never be performed when flammable gas exceeds safe limits.

---------------------------------------------------

OISD Guideline

Mandatory PPE:
Hardhat
Safety Vest
Mask

Workers violating PPE shall not enter hazardous areas.
